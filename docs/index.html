Protocol flow

Four on-chain steps.
Zero manual upkeep.

After a subscription is created, nothing requires a server, a cron job, or a custodian. The protocol runs itself.

1

Create a plan

Merchant defines price, billing interval, and an optional trial period. Stored on-chain in the registry.

2

Subscribe once

Subscriber approves a USDC allowance and calls subscribe. Billing begins immediately, or after any trial.

3

Keeper triggers billing

An autonomous keeper scans on-chain state continuously and fires billing the moment a cycle is due.

4

Revenue settles

USDC is pulled from the subscriber, the protocol fee routes automatically, and the rest credits the merchant vault.

Integrate

Three calls. You're live.

Retry logic, grace periods, and billing cycles are handled by the protocol itself — not your application code.

subscribe.ts TypeScript · viem 
// 1. Approve USDC allowance once
await wallet.writeContract({
  address: USDC,
  abi: erc20Abi,
  functionName: 'approve',
  args: [PULL_PAYMENT, parseUnits('100', 6)],
})

// 2. Subscribe to a plan
const subId = await wallet.writeContract({
  address: PULL_PAYMENT,
  abi: pullAbi,
  functionName: 'subscribe',
  args: [planId, parseUnits('100', 6)],
})
